About

I'm a Northwestern alum and current Venture for America fellow living in Baltimore, MD. Before that, I grew up in San Diego where my life was more beaches and burritos than cold winters and Old Bay. At age 18 I never expected to have a strong opinion on long underwear, but I've met some truly great people on my journey Eastward and I can't predict when I'll return home.

In my professional life I've had experiences building software products and in digital marketing. As a rising product manager, I'm excited when I get to apply customer development philosophy to create products that solve a problem and learning new technologies to make sure those products work. As an amateur digital marketer, I like using my analytical skills to optimize web marketing tactics and how to tell a compelling story. Other exciting things are social enterprise, mobile money, layman's physics, and good food.
