The Remington Chop

The Remington Chop was a first-time culinary event that took place on June 13th in the Remington neighborhood of Baltimore City. When we started, our mission was to host an event that brought people to a lesser-known neighborhood of Baltimore and give our guests a chance to learn from local chefs. We choose Remington for its burgeoning culinary scene and because of the efforts of the neighborhood to help us put the event together. As one of the event organizers, I was involved in logistics, sales, and marketing. However, my focus was to drive traffic to our website and hopefully convert some of those visitors into sales. I managed our email marketing campaign, led PR outreach, and ran our social media accounts. Ultimately, sales and marketing is a team effort and we leaned heavily on our personal networks to sell out the event.
